Commission

  • brushWhat will I draw

    Pretty much everything. NSFW, gore, romantic cuddly stuff, portraits, whatever you want. It doesn’t even have to be from a fandom, if you’ve got good references for OCs, or really good descriptions, I can work with that too.

  • portraitWhat will you get

    A high resolution (above 300 dpi) .png file.

  • paymentPayment and Price

    • * Paypal, you can send me a message for info.
    • * Depends on what you want, pretty much everything including backgrounds is negotiable depending on simplicity.

  • descriptionConditions

    • * I reserve the right to deny any commission request without reason (I’ll probably give you one, but just in case).
    • * I reserve the right to publish the final results and WIPs as part of my portfolio in a smaller resolution; and DA account, or tumblr, with a watermark naming it a commission, adding a link to you.
    • * Commissioned images are for personal use only. Please don’t alter commissioned work, or take the credit/source/signature in a piece.
    • * If you want to use the image for commercial, non-personal purposes, you need the rights to the image and the pricing will change accordingly.
    • * You don’t get the preliminary sketches as yours, you can’t give them a personal or commercial use later on.

  • question_answerHow it’ll work

    • * 2 payments (50% + 50%). One to get me drawing, until you get a first sketch, and one to get me drawing again the final result.
    • * We’ll be talking through e-mails. I’ll sent you a first preliminary sketch, just check out poses and general mood and ambiance, and once we’re settled on something we both like, I’ll start drawing the final piece (this is were the 2nd payment comes).
    • * If we never settle on a sketch, this is were we part ways (no 2nd payment needed) but you can’t use the sketch later on.
    • * There’ll be a list with the commission schedule and estimated time of completion set on my blog and DA account.
